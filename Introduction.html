Introduction

The Value of AR/VR in Education

In 1957, American cinematographer Morton Heilig created a VR system called Sensorama. But how can VR function in the classroom?


By introducing new teaching and learning techniques, virtual and augmented reality are transforming education. The ability of virtual reality to bring book content to life and increase learning's fascination and engagement is by far its most evident advantage.
